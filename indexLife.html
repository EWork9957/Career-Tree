Lifestyle

A Computer Technician, or Computer Support Technician, is responsible for maintaining computer systems within a business setting. They will install computers, routers and other hardware, update or repair existing systems and troubleshoot problems with hardware. This career should provide good employment opportunities.

Lifestyle

A database architect's job is to design and build large databases (an organized collection of data.) They set standards for operations, programming, and security of the databases to ensure they perform as intended. They will help companies grow and expand into new marketplaces and help create a vision to meet strategic goals. These professionals create and manage large and expansive electronic databases.

Lifestyle

Video game designers develop user-friendly games for computers, mobile devices, and websites by brainstorming game details, building code, and testing products for efficiency. They may create plots for games and organize ideas on flowcharts. They'll bring ideas, build prototypes, create interactive narration and develop the game's mechanics.
